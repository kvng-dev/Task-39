Transforming banking and finance for good with open APIs

With open banking, the financial ecosystem in Nigeria will accelerate as technical frictions are removed and innovation is unleashed

Open banking is underpinned by various pillars

Data Privacy

The core of open banking provides an assurance of privacy and full consent to all players

Regulation

CBN regulation on open banking provides the legal and regulatory framework for banks and fintech to share customer data in a secure and responsible manner

Operational Guidelines

CBN and the financial ecosystem have designed world-class operational guidelines for how open banking will be operated by various stakeholders

APIs

Open Banking Nigeria collaborates with the industry to establish API standard enabling swift and CBN-compliant open banking products for banks and fintechs
